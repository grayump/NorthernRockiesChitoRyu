Chito-Ryu is a traditional martial art founded by Dr. Tsuyoshi Chitose. It combines elements of the Okinawan karate styles Shuri-Te and Naha-Te with medical knowledge, focusing on character development, self-discipline, and harmony between mind, body, and spirit.
