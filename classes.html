Belt Ranking System

Progress through the ranks by demonstrating skill and dedication:

  • White Belt - Beginner
  • White-Black/Stripe Belt - 6-12 months
  • Yellow Belt - 6-12 Months
  • Orange Belt - 6-12 Months
  • Green Belt - 6-12 Months
  • Green-Black/Stripe Belt - 6-12 Months
  • Blue Belt - 1 year
  • Brown Belt - 1 year
  • Brown -Black/Stripe Belt - 2 years
  • Black Belt - a new beginning

Training Information

Our classes include kihon (basics), kata (forms) and kumite (sparring). Emphasis on proper technique, respect, and personal growth.
