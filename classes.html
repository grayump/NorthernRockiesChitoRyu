Belt Ranking System

Progress through the ranks by demonstrating skill and dedication:

  • White Belt
  • White – Black Stripe Belt
  • Yellow Belt
  • Orange Belt
  • Green Belt
  • Green – Black Stripe Belt
  • Blue Belt
  • Brown Belt
  • Brown – Black Stripe Belt
  • Black Belt

Training Information

Our classes include kihon (basics), kata (forms), and kumite (sparring), with emphasis on proper technique, respect, and personal growth.
