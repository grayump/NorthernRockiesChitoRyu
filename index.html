Welcome to Northern Rockies PG Chito-Ryu Karate Club

Traditional Karate for Kids and Adults in Prince George. Build confidence, discipline, and lifelong strength through traditional karate training in a safe and respectful dojo environment.

Why Families Choose Our Dojo

At PG Chito-Ryu, we teach traditional karate the way it was meant to be learned — with structure, respect, and steady personal growth. Our classes are designed to help students of all ages develop both physical skills and strong character.

Whether your child is just starting out, or you're a teen or adult looking for meaningful training, you'll find a welcoming place to learn and improve.

  • ✔ Small class sizes
  • ✔ Experienced, certified instructors
  • ✔ Emphasis on safety, respect, and discipline
  • ✔ Beginner-friendly for all ages
  • ✔ Low monthly fees

🥋 Youth Karate (Ages 8–14)

As children grow, karate becomes a powerful tool for personal development.

🥋 Teen and Adult Karate (Ages 15+)

For Teens and Adults karate offers lifelong fitness, stress relief, and a path to personal mastery.
Our Club focuses on:

  • Discipline and goal-setting
  • Self-confidence and resilience
  • Respect, responsibility, and perseverance

Students progress at their own pace while learning traditional techniques and forms.
