Showa

Recited at the closing of every class.

Japanese

Ware, ware, Chito-Ryu Karate-doh oh shugyo surumonowa

Tsuneni bushido seshin oh wasurezu

Wa to nin oh motte nashi

Shoshite tsutomereba kanarazu tasu

English

We who study Chito-Ryu Karate-do

Shall never forget the spirit of the samurai.

With harmony, dedication, and smart work,

We shall reach our goals.
