About Chito-Ryu Karate

History

Chito-Ryu Karate was founded by Dr. Tsuyoshi Chitose. O-Sensei studied various martial arts, developing Chito-Ryu by combining the strengths of both Shuri-Te and Naha-Te with his medical knowledge.

The name "Chito-Ryu" means "Thousand Year Style," reflecting the longevity, history, and depth of the art.

Philosophy

Chito-Ryu Karate focuses on the development of character, self-discipline, and respect. Practitioners learn not only self-defense techniques but also the importance of harmony between mind, body, and spirit.

Key principles include:

  • Respect for others and oneself
  • Perseverance and dedication
  • Balance and harmony
  • Continuous improvement
